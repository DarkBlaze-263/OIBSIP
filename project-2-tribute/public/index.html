From Engineer to Icon

1963

Born in Tainan, Taiwan. Moved to the United States as a child.

1993

Co-founded NVIDIA with Chris Malachowsky and Curtis Priem with a vision for graphics and parallel computing.

1999

NVIDIA invented the GPU and defined modern computer graphics and gaming.

Today

NVIDIA powers AI, data centers, autonomous vehicles, and the metaverse. Jensen leads as CEO with the same intensity as day one.

Legacy & Impact

  • Brought real-time ray tracing and AI to gaming and professional graphics.
  • Pioneered CUDA and GPU computing beyond graphics.
  • Made NVIDIA the backbone of modern AI training and inference.
  • Shaped culture with a focus on speed, innovation, and long-term thinking.
